FARM LAND


Kohir * Roads Kaveli village Kohir * Roads .Sanagareddy,Hydrabad to mumbai Highway

Farm land constitutes a crucial and dynamic element within the agricultural domain, providing the canvas for a range of cultivation and husbandry practices. These extensive parcels of land, often categorized into distinct types based on their agricultural functions, play a fundamental role in sustaining food production, facilitating livestock farming, and contributing to the overall economic landscape of a region. Enriched with fertile soils, arable land serves as the nurturing ground for a variety of crops, while pasture and grazing land support livestock, playing a pivotal role in the production of meat and dairy products. Orchards, defined by orderly rows of fruit-bearing trees, yield abundant harvests, and ranch land provides the setting for large-scale livestock operations. The layout of farm land displays a wide variety, ranging from expansive fields of grain to verdant pastures and meticulously planned orchards.

Namavaram village, Near Satilite city , Morumpudi junction

The region around Rajahmundry is known for its agricultural activities, and there are numerous farmlands in and around the city. Farmlands in the Rajahmundry region are typically used for cultivating a variety of crops, including rice, sugarcane, coconut, banana, and other fruits and vegetables. The Godavari Delta, which is nearby, is known for its fertile soil, making it suitable for agriculture. The agriculture in this region is influenced by the Godavari River, and the availability of water from the river is crucial for irrigation. Farmers in the area often depend on the river for their agricultural needs.
